# archive

Where things go once they're done: processed Raw Captures (post-triage, post-synthesis) and completed or abandoned projects.

**What the Engine/Adapters expect to find here:**

- Processed raw captures, moved out of `../inbox/raw/` once triaged and, where relevant, compiled into the Wiki — kept for provenance, not re-processed.
- Completed/abandoned project folders, moved out of `../projects/` unchanged.
- Nothing here is read by Routines in the normal flow; it's history, not an active surface.

Empty at template-clone time.
