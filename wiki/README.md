# wiki

The machine-compiled, concept-organised knowledge layer — a pure function of Raw Captures plus validated feedback. Never directly human-edited; the Librarian is the sole writer.

**What the Engine/Adapters expect to find here:**

- One article per concept, compiled and kept current by the Librarian's Compile/Audit/Tune loop.
- The **resynthesis guarantee**: this entire folder can be dropped and rebuilt from `../inbox/raw/` (once archived) plus feedback, at any time, by any model, with no human work lost. A safety property, not a determinism claim — rebuilds may differ in wording.
- Articles link back to the Raw Captures and Action Log entries they were derived from, and forward into `../areas/` where a decision was made off the back of them.

Empty at template-clone time — the Librarian populates it as captures accumulate.
